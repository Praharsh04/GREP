    Given: The mean of three numbers 2x2x, 3x3x, x2x2 is 1212

    So we can write: 2x+3x+x23=122x+3x+x23=12

    Simplify the numerator: x2+5x3=12x2+5x3=12

    Multiply both sides of the equation by 33 to get: x2+5x=36x2+5x=36

    Subtract 3636 from both sides: x2+5x−36=0x2+5x−36=0

    Factor: (x+9)(x−4)=0(x+9)(x−4)=0

    So, either x=−9x=−9 or x=4x=4

    Since we are told x<0x<0, we can be certain that x=−9x=−9

    If x=−9x=−9, then the values of the three numbers are as follows:

    2x=2(−9)=−182x=2(−9)=−18
    3x=3(−9)=−273x=3(−9)=−27
    x2=(−9)2=81x2=(−9)2=81

    Range = (largest number) - (smallest number)
    =81−(−27)=81−(−27)
    =108=108

    Answer: A
